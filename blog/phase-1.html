    Dev Bootcamp

    It's the end of the second week of Phase 1 at DBC yet it seems like it's been forever. Let's go back to day 1..

    Phase 1, Week 1, Day 1

    The first day was a "meet everyone" day with free breakfast and lunch. It began in the building's lobby and signing in as a visitor until the mentors came down to pass out name tags. Afterwards, they brought us up to meet the other cohorts and everyone else at DBC. Spent a long time bouncing from introductions to advice the other students had for us about how to handle DBC. Once that was over, our phase leader and instructors for our cohort, led us to the Phase 1 area and explained how things work and whatnot (such as storing groceries in the kitchen, gaining access into the building, etc.). We finally got to "work" around 3:30pm or so.

    The first day was stressful for me. It's a ton of socializing and at the end of all that, we had to complete 2 challenges for the day (exactly like the challenges from phase 0).
